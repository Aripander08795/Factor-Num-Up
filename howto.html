Factor Num Up
How to play?

Factors
Factors can increase the production of number multiplicatively and can be stacked. You can increase the factor additively by spending with number.
Every factor have level 1 when you start this game. Each time you spend with number to increase factor, it gains a level.

Prime
Prime is the currency which you can earn by embracing the power (in this game, this means soft reset). If you embrace the power, only number and factors resets.
You can spend with these to unlock more features.

Upgrades
Upgrades is a first feature. The cost of this feature is free; so don't worry, you don't lose prime to unlock upgrades!
If you spent with prime to buy an upgrade, that upgrade boosts your production of number or increases one of 7 factors.
